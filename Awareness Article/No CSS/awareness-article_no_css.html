Introduction

COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, is a global pandemic affecting millions worldwide. This article provides an overview, symptoms, and essential precautions.
In late 2019, the world witnessed the emergence of a novel coronavirus, COVID-19, which rapidly evolved into a global pandemic.
Declared a Public Health Emergency of International Concern by the World Health Organization (WHO) in January 2020, COVID-19 has spread to every region, infecting
millions and claiming countless lives. The pandemic's unprecedented scale and velocity have tested the resilience of healthcare systems, economies, and societies worldwide.
As governments, healthcare professionals, and individuals continue to grapple with the virus's devastating consequences, understanding the pandemic's causes, effects,
and responses is crucial for mitigating its impact and shaping a post-pandemic future.

Symptoms

  1. Fever
  2. Cough
  3. Shortness of breath
  4. Fatigue
  5. Headache

Precautions

  1. Wear masks
  2. Maintain social distancing
  3. Wash hands frequently
  4. Avoid touching face
  5. Stay home if symptomatic

Vaccination and Treatment

COVID-19 vaccines and treatments are available. Consult healthcare professionals for guidance.

Conclusion

Stay informed, follow guidelines, and prioritize health to combat the COVID-19 pandemic.
